### What About Hate, Injustice, Weakness, Foolishness?

#### Three Models of Opposition

**Model 1: Symmetric Dualism**
- Love and Hate are equal, opposite forces
- Justice and Injustice are co-equal powers
- Reality is battle between light and darkness
- **Problem**: Predicts equal power, but empirically Love amplifies other dimensions (κ_LJ = 1.4), while hate diminishes. Asymmetric.

**Model 2: Privation Theory** (Classical Christian/Augustinian)
- Evil is not a "thing" but an **absence** of good
- Hate is absence of Love
- Injustice is absence of Justice
- Darkness is absence of light (not a substance)
- **Advantage**: Explains why evil is always parasitic, never creative

**Model 3: Inversion Theory**
- Opposites are **inversions** or **corruptions** of primaries
- Hate is Love turned inward (narcissism) or misdirected
- Injustice is Justice applied partially (favoritism)
- **Advantage**: Explains how evil mimics good

## The Bootstrap Paradox

### The Circularity Problem

If God has LJPW attributes, and LJPW exists because God exists, **where did God get LJPW?**

This is the classical "bootstrap" problem in theology: What grounds God's goodness? Is God good because goodness is higher than God (Platonism), or is goodness good because God defines it (Divine Command Theory)?

### Resolution: Identity Theory

The answer is **identity**: God does not "have" LJPW attributes, God **IS** LJPW.

```
Love is not something God possesses → Love is what God is
Justice is not something God does → Justice is God's nature
Power is not a capability God has → Power is God's being
Wisdom is not knowledge God acquired → Wisdom is God's essence
```

**There is no gap between God and LJPW.** They are not two things (God + LJPW), but one reality seen from two angles:

- **Theologically**: JEHOVAH, the self-existent One
- **Mathematically**: (1, 1, 1, 1), perfect balance of primaries

This resolves the bootstrap paradox: LJPW doesn't ground God, and God doesn't ground LJPW. **They are identical.**

When Moses asks God's name (Exodus 3:14), God replies:

This is not evasion. It's **ontological declaration**:

If LJPW = God's nature, and God = I AM (pure being), then:
